Max Temperature

This is the City Latitude vs. Max Temperature scatter plot. The plot is displaying the relationship between a city's latitude and its max temp on the randomly chosen date of 1/5/17. As shown in the scatter plot, distance from the equator and temperature are strongly correlated, specifically in the Northern Hemisphere (lat>0).
