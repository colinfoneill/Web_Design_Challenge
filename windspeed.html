Wind Speed

This is the City Latitude vs. Wind Speed scatter plot. The plot is displaying the relationship between a city's latitude and its wind speed (mph) on the randomly chosen date of 1/5/17. As shown in the scatter plot, there is a moderate correlation between city latitude and wind speed, especially in the far northern hemisphere where the majority of the windy cities appear to lie.
